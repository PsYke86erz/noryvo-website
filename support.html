FAQ

How do I create an account?
Enter your phone number and verify with the SMS code.

How do I delete my account?
Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account.

How do I export my data?
Go to Profile → Settings → Export Data (GDPR compliant).

Is my data secure?
Yes. All connections are encrypted with TLS 1.3. Servers are in Germany.
